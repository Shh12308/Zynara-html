Careers at Zynara

Join the Zynara Journey

Zynara is a fast-growing AI project with a vision to revolutionize how people interact with AI. While this project started as an independent initiative, we are always open to collaboration with talented and passionate individuals.

Opportunities

  • AI research collaborators
  • Frontend and backend developers
  • Creative writers for AI training and blog content
  • Community managers and outreach
  • Internships for students passionate about AI and technology

Why Work With Us?

Working with Zynara means being part of a passionate, innovative, and ambitious project from the ground up. You’ll gain hands-on experience, help shape the AI community, and contribute to the development of next-generation AI systems.

We are also building our own proprietary AI LLM and advanced multimodal systems.

Compensation & Payments

Zynara is currently an independent and self-funded AI project. Compensation depends on the nature of the collaboration, project scope, and funding stage.

  • Paid roles: Available for select development, infrastructure, and design tasks.
  • Revenue sharing: Early contributors may receive revenue-based or long-term incentive agreements.
  • Bounties: One-time payments for completed features, integrations, or research milestones.
  • Internships & collaborations: Learning-focused roles with potential future paid opportunities.

All compensation is discussed transparently in advance. Supported payment methods may include bank transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency where applicable.

As Zynara grows and monetization expands, we aim to transition more roles into fully paid positions. Early contributors will be prioritized.
